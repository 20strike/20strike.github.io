Welcome to 20strike!

We’re here to have fun, compete, and enjoy some friendly CS:GO matches and wagers. Let’s keep it respectful and exciting for everyone!

1. Respect Each Other

Treat everyone with kindness. No harassment, hate speech, or toxic behavior.

2. Keep it Friendly Competition

Trash talk is part of the game, but keep it light-hearted.

3. No Cheating or Hacking

Play fair! Any form of cheating, hacking, or exploiting bugs is strictly prohibited.

4. Follow Wagering Guidelines

All wagers must be agreed upon by both parties beforehand and must be honored.

5. Use Appropriate Channels

Keep the server organized by using the right channels for the right topics.

6. No Spamming or Self-Promotion

Avoid spamming messages or promoting your own content without permission.

7. Respect Privacy

Do not share personal information without consent.

8. Listen to Moderators

Mods are here to help and keep the peace.

9. Have Fun!

This is the most important rule. Enjoy CS:GO, make friends, and have a great time.
